Ministries

Youth ministry

At Ignite, we believe that young people are the future of the church and have a unique role to play in shaping the world around them. Our goal is to equip them with the tools, resources, and support they need to become leaders and agents of change in their communities. Our youth ministry programs are designed to be fun, engaging, and relevant to the needs and interests of young people today. We offer a variety of activities and events, such as retreats, service projects, social outings, and small group Bible studies, that provide opportunities for young people to connect with each other and with God.

Kids

Our church mission is to love God, love people, and make disciples. his ministry provides a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn about God, the Bible, and their faith in an age-appropriate way. The primary goal of children's ministry is to introduce children to Jesus Christ and help them develop a personal relationship with Him. This is typically accomplished through a variety of activities, such as Bible lessons, worship, prayer, fellowship, and service projects.

Choir

Neema Gospel Choir is a youth choir ministry that was founded in 2022. The choir comprises young singers who come together to lead worship and share the gospel message through music. Their purpose is to glorify God through music, by performing a variety of styles including hymns, contemporary Christian music, and gospel. The choir provides an opportunity for young people to use their talents and gifts to serve in ministry and form strong bonds with one another. Neema Gospel Choir also has a role in evangelism, as they perform at local events to reach those who may not attend church regularly..
