Meet our senior pastor

Pastor Josh is a compassionate and wise spiritual leader who serves as the head of his church. He delivers inspiring sermons, offers pastoral care to individuals and families, and works with other community leaders to promote social justice and provide community services. With his ability to connect with people from all walks of life, Pastor Josh is a source of strength, hope, and inspiration for his congregation..

Associate pastor

Pastor Mercy is a spiritual leader in her own right, serving alongside her husband, Pastor Josh, as a pastor in their church. With her deep knowledge of scripture and compassionate nature, she provides guidance and support to members of their congregation. Pastor Mercy is dedicated to helping individuals and families deepen their faith, find purpose and meaning, and navigate life's challenges. Her presence and leadership within the church community are invaluable, as she works with Pastor Josh and other leaders to promote social justice and provide essential community services. Together, Pastor Josh and Pastor Mercy form a powerful and inspiring pastoral team, serving their congregation with dedication, compassion, and faith.
